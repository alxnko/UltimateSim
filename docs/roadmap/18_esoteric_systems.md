# Phase 18: Esoteric Systems & Ideological Apex

_Objective: Push the simulation to its final logic expansion by introducing highly abstract concepts—Religion, Esoteric Physics (Magic), and absolute modding freedom over the physical array constraints._

## 18.1 Ideological Warfare

- **`PreacherSystem`:** Expand the `GossipDistributionSystem` from Phase 7. Specific NPCs holding `JobComponent(Preacher)` actively override target `BeliefComponent` weights across vast HPA\* Regions, rather than just localized adjacent grid tiles.
- **Holy Wars:** If a `CityID`'s aggregate `BeliefComponent` differs completely from a neighboring `CityID`, override peaceful proximity modifiers and generate aggressive physical `Entity` spawns targeting rival storage clusters.

## 18.2 Abstract Physics (The "Magic" Expansion)

- Because the ECS strictly separates logic from data, introducing "Magic" simply means injecting a new numerical system.
- **`ManaComponent` limit threshold:** Add to `MapData` arrays.
- **`CastingSystem`:** Specific NPCs read `ManaComponent` and execute explicit `SystemOverrides` (e.g., instantly destroying a `Caravan` or artificially spiking the local `Temperature` variable on the MapGrid). This acts as the final proof of the "Lego Principle" scalability.
